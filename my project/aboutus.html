About Us

  ‘Sphoorthy’ is the embodiment of inspiration and the fundamental objective of our institution. Our aim is to provide a stimulating atmosphere for young minds to reform and recreate their potentials to break the barrier of success. We at Sphoorthy Engineering College emphasize promoting quality education, independent thought process, and overall personality development of the students. The campus has a vibrant environment to accommodate students from diverse backgrounds. At Sphoorthy Engineering College, we assure you a life filled with fun, growth, and life-changing memories.

History

  The institution was established in 2004 with the approval from AICTE. It has a permanent affiliation with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad. ‘Sphoorthy Engineering College’ has been accredited by NAAC for holding a promising record of academic excellence and achievements. The institution provides state of the art infrastructural facilities for promoting technological advancement and innovation. Over the years, the institution has been working for the betterment of student’s future by empowering them with deep conceptual knowledge, wide technical training opportunities and also by creating a strong platform for strengthening their innovative and creative skills. The institution has progressed impeccably in providing best placements for the students every year.
